The Keynote Session speaker was Dr. Mukta Paliwal, an AWS certified ML Specialist associated with Persistent Systems for five years where she has handled various roles including Project Lead, Senior Team Lead, Consultant Analytics, etc.

The topic of the Panel Discussion was: "PREPARING FOR SUCCESSFUL CAREERS IN AI, ML AND DATA SCIENCE".

The Panelists were:
  • Ms Shachi Dave, a Software Engineer in the Natural Language Understanding group at Google Research India.
  • Mr Anudeep Jain. He is managing development for treasury solution and is the product owner for business in integration with FIS flagship product called Front Arena.
  • Ms Neelima Vobugari, currently CEO of Tarah Technologies, a marquee AI and Machine Learning competency and consulting firm. She is a certified CRM consultant and a certified Data Scientist.
  • Dr Kalika Bali, a researcher at Microsoft Research India working in the areas of Machine Learning, Natural Language Systems and Applications, as well as Technology for Emerging Markets.
